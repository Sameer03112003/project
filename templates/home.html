Getting Started

Click on the Create Account button and fill out the registration form with your details. Once registered, you can immediately log in and start using the system.

After logging in, navigate to your Account page. You'll find a deposit form where you can enter the amount and description for your deposit.

Go to the Transfer page, enter the recipient's username and the amount you want to send. The transfer will be processed instantly.

Managers have access to the Manager Dashboard where they can view, edit, and delete users. Contact an administrator to get manager privileges.
